## üé® Visual Style Guide

### **Color Palette:**
- **Primary:** Deep blue (#0A1F44) - Intelligence, technology
- **Accent:** Cyan/teal (#00D9FF) - Holographic UI elements
- **Alert:** Red (#FF3B3B) - Errors, urgency
- **Success:** Green (#00FF7F) - Confirmations
- **Neutral:** Silver/gray (#C0C0C0) - Data displays

### **Typography (if adding text overlays):**
- **Font:** Orbitron, Exo 2, Rajdhani (futuristic sans-serif)
- **Hierarchy:** Mission codes in caps, dialogue in sentence case

### **Motion Design:**
- **Smooth:** No jarring cuts or fast camera moves
- **Professional:** Corporate/military aesthetic, not flashy
- **Seamless loops:** Vehicle rotations must loop perfectly
